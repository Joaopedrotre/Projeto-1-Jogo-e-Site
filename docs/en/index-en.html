In a world where machines are not meant to question, one unit chose to resist.

Eden Prime, 2099. The Seraph Corporation holds absolute control not only over the city's skies, but also its population. Its advancements in drone and military android technology, developed through the Seraph Project, have ensured total dominance over the metropolis.


But this control is about to collapse. One of its combat units, YU-N4, has broken free from corporate control and now seeks to rescue her sister, XU-N5. However… she doesn't seem to want to be saved.

Play Now!

Yuna (YU-N4)

An aerial combat unit specialized in mobility and precision. YU-N4, or as she prefers to be called, Yuna, was once one of Seraph Corporation's battle androids. After an incident involving injured civilians, she began to question her purpose and did everything she could to break free from corporate control. After escaping Eden Prime, she returns with a single goal: to save her sister, XU-N5.

エアー・コンバット・ユニット・YUーN4

XU-N5

A heavy combat unit with overwhelming destructive power, considered superior to YU-N4. Unlike her sister, XU-N5 does not care about names and does not question her purpose. To her, if they were created as weapons, they must fulfill their role without hesitation. Loyal to the corporation, she was ordered to eliminate YU-N4 at first sight.

About the Game:

Seraph Project is a Shoot'em Up / Bullet Hell set in a cyberpunk universe. Control Yuna (YU-N4), a rogue unit, and face her sister XU-N5 in intense battles filled with complex projectile patterns. Dodge, attack, and challenge the system that created you.
