インスピレーション

Touhou Project

Touhou Project is a well-known Japanese indie game franchise in the Bullet Hell genre, famous for its complex projectile patterns and high level of difficulty. Players must shoot and defeat enemies while dodging what often feels like a “rain of bullets” across the screen, requiring fast reflexes, precision, and pattern memorization.


The franchise gained massive popularity not only because of its games, but especially due to its extremely active fan culture. Fans create a vast amount of derivative content (doujin), including remixed music, animations, manga, and even fan-made games. The series has over 30 official titles, almost all of which were developed by a single person: its creator, ZUN.

Yu-Gi-Oh: Sky Striker

The Sky Striker cards from the Yu-Gi-Oh! trading card game stand out for their visual identity, heavily inspired by sci-fi anime, with a strong focus on advanced technology and futuristic aesthetics.


The artwork revolves around a central character (Raye), equipped with different technological armors that change in form and function. Each version represents a distinct visual style, while consistently featuring elements such as energy wings, glowing particles, and digital effects.
