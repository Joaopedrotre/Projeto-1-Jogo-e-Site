Movement

Move Yuna in up to 8 directions across the screen using the arrow keys to dodge enemy projectiles. Precise movement is essential, as even small decisions can make a huge difference when navigating through bullet patterns.

Shooting

Yuna fires while holding the Z key. Use your attacks to hit XU-N5 and her drones, but remember: you won't always be able to deal damage to her while dodging. Survival always comes first.

Focus Mode

Hold Shift to reduce movement speed, allowing for more precise dodging. Focus mode also reveals the character's hitbox, showing exactly how close you can get without being hit.

Bombs

Bombs act as a limited emergency resource, clearing all projectiles on screen and dealing damage to XU-N5. Use them at the right moment to escape dangerous situations.
